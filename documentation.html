Bot Configuration Guide

Customize the AI's behavior using the toggle switches found in the control panel.

Strategy Modifiers

  • Counter Trade

    Reverses the AI's decision (Long becomes Short).
    Use when: Market is ranging/choppy and breakouts are failing.

  • AI Flip

    Allows the bot to close a position and open the opposite side immediately if sentiment shifts.
    Use when: High volatility scalping is desired.

  • Dynamic Config

    AI decides the leverage and position size based on its confidence score.
    Use when: You want to maximize gains on high-conviction setups.

  • Custom Prompt

    Injects specialized instructions into the AI context.
    Advanced users only.

Risk Management

  • Dynamic TP

    Calculates Take Profit targets based on market volatility (ATR) rather than fixed percentages.
    Recommended: Always ON for optimized exits.

  • Hard Stop Loss

    Places a physical Stop Loss order on the exchange book.
    Mandatory for: Leverage > 20x or unattended bots.

  • Recheck TP

    When TP is hit, AI re-evaluates. If the trend is still strong, it holds for more profit.
    Use when: Catching parabolic moves.

  • FGI Thresholds

    Filters trades based on Fear & Greed Index.
    Example: Only buy when Fear < 38 or Greed > 62.

System Parameters

Global settings that affect how the AI engine operates. Accessible via the settings cog icon.

AI Creativity (Temp)

Controls the randomness of the LLM. Lower (0.1-0.3) is more deterministic/analytical. Higher (0.7+) is more creative but risky.

Analysis Freq

Time interval (in minutes) between market scans when no position is open.

Cooldown

Mandatory wait time (in minutes) after closing a position before opening a new one to prevent overtrading.

Flip Check Freq

How often (in minutes) the bot checks for a reversal signal while a position is open.

Flip Confidence

The minimum confidence level (Low, Medium, High, Very High) required to trigger an AI Flip.

AI Personas

Concise (Default)

Balanced approach. Good for general purpose trading. Focuses on clear market structure breaks.

Detailed

Deep analysis. Considers more factors. Slower to react but filters out noise better.

Skeptical

Risk-averse. Requires high confirmation. Misses some trades but has higher win rate.

Aggressive

High frequency. Takes trades on lower confidence. Good for catching wicks and scalps.
